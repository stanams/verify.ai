About Verify.ai

Verify.ai offers a cutting-edge solution to counter deep fakes through the use of steganography and perceptual hashes. Designed for public figures frequently targeted by fake media portrayals, our service ensures the authenticity of media content against scams or discrediting attempts.

How It Works

Media Signing by Public Figures

Public figures can authenticate their media (videos, sounds, images) through Verify.ai. This involves processing the content using perceptual hashes to maintain integrity after compression, followed by digital signing with asymmetric cryptography. The signed hashes and the public figure's public key are then embedded into the media file using steganography, preserving the original perceptibility.

Content Sharing and Verification

Once embedded, the content can be shared across platforms. End-users can verify authenticity by extracting and validating the embedded perceptual hashes and public key against a dedicated certificate authority for public figures. A matching hash confirms the media's authenticity.
